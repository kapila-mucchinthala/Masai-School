Working and thriving

We want people to thrive at Slack; we believe you do your best work when
you feel your best. Here are a just a few benefits we're proud to provide our
team with.

Learning & development

Receive an annual allowencefor professional
development, plus a monthly bonus you can use
towards anything. That meansanything: a gym
membership, your pet sitter, anew work look.
Whatever matters most to you.

Health & wellness

Free medical, dental care and eye care for you
and your family - Slack pays 100% of your
premiums.

Parental leave

A lot of us have families, of all sorts of shapes
and sizes, and we welcome each new addition
with generous parental leave and "bonding
leave".

Happier weekdays

A culture light on meetings (or that's our aim, at
least) with guest speakers and opportunities to
get to know your team better through events
like weekly catered lunches and breakfasts,
thursday Socials and game nights. But we don't
stay in the office 24/7.
